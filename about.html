About Bean Haven

Our story, our passion, our people.

Who We Are

Bean Haven was founded in 2015 by Emma and Raj, two coffee enthusiasts who dreamed of creating a neighborhood café that felt like a second home. What started as a tiny corner shop has grown into one of the most loved spots in the city.

We believe great coffee is about more than the bean — it's about the experience. From the moment you walk in, we want you to feel welcomed, comfortable, and a little bit happier.

Our Values

  • 🌱 Sustainability – We compost, recycle, and support eco-friendly suppliers.
  • 🤝 Community – We partner with local farms and host neighborhood events.
  • 💛 Quality – Every dish and drink is crafted with care and the best ingredients.
